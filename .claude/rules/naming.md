# **Naming Conventions**

## **Frontend (React/JS)**

* **Components:** PascalCase (例: Sidebar.jsx, FileViewer.jsx)  
* **Functions/Variables:** camelCase (例: fetchFileList, selectedTab)  
* **Constants:** UPPER\_SNAKE\_CASE (例: DEFAULT\_THEME)

## **Backend (Python)**

* **Functions/Variables:** snake\_case (例: update\_list\_json, file\_content)  
* **Constants:** UPPER\_SNAKE\_CASE (例: BUCKET\_NAME)

## **File Naming**

* **Components:** PascalCase  
* **Hooks/Utils:** camelCase  
* **Markdown Files:** kebab-case (例: project-overview.md)
